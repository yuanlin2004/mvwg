Our San Diego event (2/20–2/21) was a meaningful milestone for Monta Vista-Lynbrook Winter Guard.

While we did not advance to finals this time, this was a highly competitive event, and for about 90% of our students, it was their first travel competition. We are very proud of how our students represented themselves and the team with energy, courage, and spirit.

Beyond the performance floor, the weekend gave us unforgettable team moments: touring the UC San Diego campus on day one and sharing a warm hotel pot-luck together.

Special thanks to **Lisa** and **Brian** for chaperoning and helping make this trip smooth, safe, and positive for everyone.

This trip was about growth, experience, and building confidence at a high level. We learned a lot, had fun, and we’re taking that momentum into the next performance.
